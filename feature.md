## Example format

- **age**: Age of the customer in years. Older customers may have different purchasing patterns. Numeric, typically 18-95.
- **tenure**: Number of months the customer has been with the company. Higher tenure usually means lower churn risk. Numeric, 0-72.
- **MonthlyCharges**: The amount charged to the customer each month in dollars. Numeric, 18.25-118.75.
- **Contract**: Type of contract — Month-to-month, One year, or Two year. Month-to-month customers churn at much higher rates.
